## Short Explanation
Introducing AIGET, a revolutionary note-taking application that redefines your documentation experience with the power of AI. This isn't just another note-taking app; it's a smart companion that understands and organizes your thoughts efficiently. Our standout feature is the AI-powered summarizer, leveraging OpenAI's advanced algorithms. This tool distills your notes into concise summaries, making information retrieval quick and easy. Whether you're a student, professional, or avid note-taker, AIGET is designed to streamline your note management with intelligence and precision.

## Project Goals
Our primary goal is to enhance the note-taking process by integrating cutting-edge AI technology. This involves two key objectives: firstly, to provide an intuitive and efficient platform for users to capture and organize their thoughts; and secondly, to implement an AI-powered summarizer feature that simplifies the review and comprehension of notes. AIGET is committed to transforming how you interact with your notes, offering both simplicity and depth in your documentation journey.

## Features
- Advanced Note-Taking Interface
- AI-Powered Summarizer leveraging OpenAI
- Easy Organization and Retrieval of Notes
- Seamless User Experience for Various User Groups

## Tech Stack Used
* **Flutter**: The core of our application, providing a responsive and dynamic user interface.
* **OpenAI API**: Integrating OpenAI's advanced algorithms for our AI summarizer feature, providing accurate and concise summaries of user notes.
