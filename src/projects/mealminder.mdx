## Short Explanation
MealMinder is your personal eating companion. Tired of irregular eating habits? MealMinder is here to help. Our app reminds you to eat at your preferred times and even generates a customized eating schedule based on your preferences. Never skip a meal again with MealMinder!

## Project Goals
This project aims to help people with irregular eating habit by making them a healthy meal reminder. This project was designed to be a tool that can support them to change their eating habit slow and steady.

## Tech Stack Used
* Swift UI : This app was made entirely with swiftUI for its interfaces and app logic.
