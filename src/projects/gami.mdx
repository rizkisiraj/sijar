## Short Explanation
Welcome to GAMI, your ultimate thrift shop e-commerce destination where giving and making an impact collide! Our website is not just a place to find unique treasures at affordable prices but also features a user-friendly chatbot to guide you seamlessly through your shopping experience. On the admin side, harness the power of AI-supported analytics for strategic marketing insights. GAMI, where every purchase is an opportunity to give back and make a positive impact.

## Project Goals
Our project goals are twofold: first, to assist customers in finding the perfect fit for their needs when navigating our thrift shop e-commerce website, thanks to our intuitive chatbot; and second, to empower administrators with AI-supported analytics, aiding them in crafting the right business strategy for optimal leverage and success.

## Features
- AI-Powered Chatbot
- Marketing, CFM, Review Analysis using AI

## Tech Stack Used
* <b>Laravel</b> : This website was made entirely with laravel and its blade template
* <b>OpenAI API</b> : This website used OpenAI API as the barebone of the analysis and the customer chatbot.
